Space Agencies & Organizations

NASA

The United States space agency responsible for missions like Hubble, JWST, Mars rovers, and deep-space exploration.

ESA (European Space Agency)

Europe’s space organization, known for missions such as Gaia, Rosetta, and Euclid.

JPL (Jet Propulsion Laboratory)

A NASA center designing robotic spacecraft including Voyager, Cassini, and Perseverance.

SETI Institute

Focused on the scientific search for extraterrestrial life using radio signals and astrobiology.

ESO (European Southern Observatory)

Operates powerful telescopes in Chile, producing detailed cosmic images and data.

Astrobiology & Life in the Universe

Astrobiology communities study how life begins, evolves, and might exist beyond Earth.

Recent Discoveries

  • New exoplanets found in habitable zones
  • JWST revealing early galaxies
  • Gaia mapping billions of stars
  • Organic molecules on Enceladus
  • Fast radio bursts from distant galaxies
