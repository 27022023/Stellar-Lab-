About Stellar Lab

A digital space museum exploring the universe through images, science, and storytelling.

What Is Stellar Lab?

Stellar Lab is an online astronomy project that brings together cosmic images, clear explanations, and themed exhibits to make the universe feel closer and more understandable.

Mission

The mission of Stellar Lab is to spark curiosity, share knowledge, and inspire people to learn more about space, science, and the possibility of life beyond Earth.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Exhibits on planetary systems, stars and nebulae, black holes, and life in the universe
  • High-quality space imagery from observatories and scientific missions
  • Simple, structured explanations of complex cosmic phenomena
  • A growing collection of topics and discoveries in astronomy

Created By

Stellar Lab is created by Rafailia, combining a passion for astronomy, creativity, and a love of learning. Each page is crafted to make the cosmos feel welcoming and exciting.

Future Plans

As Stellar Lab grows, more exhibits, interactive features, animations, timelines, and community content will be added to expand this digital universe.
