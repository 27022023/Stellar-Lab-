What Are Stellar Lab Experiments?

Experiments in astronomy help us understand how planets form, how stars evolve, how black holes bend space, and how life might emerge on distant worlds. Because we cannot touch stars or travel to exoplanets, scientists use simulations to recreate cosmic physics safely and accurately.

Why Simulations Matter

Simulations allow us to model gravity, light, motion, and chemistry across enormous scales. They reveal how galaxies collide, how planets orbit, how atmospheres behave, and how life‑friendly environments might form.

What You Can Do Here

In this lab, you can explore interactive models, run simple physics demos, visualize cosmic phenomena, and test scientific ideas. Each simulation is designed to be clear, safe, and fun — perfect for curious explorers.
